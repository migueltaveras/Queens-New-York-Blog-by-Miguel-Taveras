Welcome to Queens





My name is Miguel Taveras and Queens, New York has been my home for 37 years. Some say Queens, New York is the most ethnically diverse area in the world. With many great parks and restaurants I decided to write a blog about a place I proudly call home!

My Favorite Parks in Queens:

Queens is home to many great neighborhoods like Corona, Astoria, Long Island City to name a few.


Flushing Meadows Corona Park


Gantry State Park-Long Island City


Astoria Park



Parks Locations:

Queens has many historic landmarks and great parks, including Flushing Meadows Corona Park, which is located in Corona,Queens. Astoria park is also a great place to visit, located in Astoria, it has amazing views by the water. Gantry State Park , also pictured above, in Long Island City, has views of the Manhattan skyline and is home to many great restaurants with amazing food as well.


Great Restaurants:

Queens is known to have alot of great restaurants and places to eat. Since its the 2nd largest borough in New york State, there are many great options and restaurants to choose from! Below are a few restaurants that offer great food:


La Cabana

La Cabana located in Corona, Queens offers amazing Dominican food that will have you coming back for more.

Blend on the Water

Blend on the water is located in Long Island City, a few blocks away from Gantry State Park, as mentioned above. It offers fine dining and has great food and views as well.

Basil Brick Oven Pizza

Basil Brick Oven Pizza is one of my favorite pizzerias in queens, it is located in Astoria, Queens and serves some of the best pizza you can find in New York!
